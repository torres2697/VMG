Veritas Management Group (VMG) is a leading management consulting firm offering commercial, defense and federal, healthcare and management consulting services to government organizations, the military, private corporations, and nonprofit organizations. Our diverse team of experts stay on top of the details, so our clients can focus on the big picture.

Our Services

Military
Support

We offer program services to support mission readiness, including IT, training, communications, data analytics, leadership training, and administrative support. We also provide support for religious services, including musicians and religious coordinators.

Research |
Evaluation

We specialize in designing and implementing domestic and global public health process and outcome evaluations, assessing agency capacity, monitoring program progress, and managing and analyzing data to ensure that the progress and impact are visible.

Data Analytics | Information Technology

We design and execute solutions for complex business problems. Our reports, dashboards, and infographics help interpret complex data sets into actionable findings. Our data analysts and engineers cover data management, mining, testing, validation, and process improvement. We support SAS and SharePoint, as well as web and software development and management.

International Support

We support global public health programs by providing monitoring and evaluation, epidemiology, surveillance, financial and operations management, technical assistance, and training.

Administrative Support

Our dedicated administrative team ensures your operations run smoothly and efficiently. Our services include expert calendar management, task management, project liaising, and flawless internal communication flow to keep your operations on point.

Leadership Development

We plan, implement, and enhance your organizational structure. We help expand your leaders’ capacity to perform their roles effectively by offering executive coaching and training. This helps them to develop their teams’ ability to execute the organization’s strategy. We also offer business process improvement services to enhance the efficiency of your processes and operations.

Health Communication

We specialize in effective communication across the public health sector via digital, social, and public relations channels. We develop, review, and manage web content and scientific literature with the goal of informing and inspiring positive action.

Health Equity | Diversity & Inclusion

We provide comprehensive health equity, diversity, and inclusion services to support the Healthy People 2030 Initiative. Our services include literature reviews; evaluation of equity, cultural competency and CLAS standards; data collection and analysis to identify the social impact of health programs; diversity training; coaching; and leadership and organizational development.
